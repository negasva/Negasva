Our Story

NEGASVA was born with a simple idea: to transform people's photos into personalized portraits of their favorite cartoons. What started as a personal project became a mission to create unique, quality art for each customer.

Each portrait is carefully designed by hand by our team of dedicated artists, ensuring it captures not only your physical appearance, but also your personality and style.
